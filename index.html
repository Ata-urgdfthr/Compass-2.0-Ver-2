Gather your documents

This is the student document set for an Extension of Stay. Collect each item, then file it yourself at Immigration — we make sure you arrive ready.

ℹ️ Immigration forms are legally sensitive, so Compass guides you step-by-step rather than auto-filling them. You review, sign, and submit yourself.
Enrollment certificate (在学証明書)
Request it from your university's student office.
Transcript / academic record
Shows Immigration you're progressing in your studies.
Attendance record
Immigration specifically checks attendance for students.
Passport & residence card
Plus one recent photo (4×3 cm).
Change of status

Collect employer documents

You're changing from Student to a work status. Most of these come from your new employer, so request them early — HR usually has a ready packet for visa changes.

📅 Timing matters. Apply about 3 months before you start work, while your student status is still valid. This is guided, not auto-filled — you submit it yourself.
Certificate of employment
From your employer's HR team.
Contract & job description
The role must match the work-visa category.
Company registration & financials
HR usually provides these as a set.
Your graduation certificate
